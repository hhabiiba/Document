Serene Reflections at Dusk

Captured during the tranquil moments of twilight, 'Serene Reflections at Dusk' portrays the serene beauty of a calm lake nestled in the embrace of a lush forest. The golden hues of the setting sun paint the sky with warm, soft tones, casting a breathtaking reflection on the tranquil waters below. This photograph evokes a sense of peace and tranquility, inviting you to immerse yourself in the serenity of nature's embrace.

  • Date: Captured on September 23, 2023.
  • Photographer: Emily Smith.
  • Camera: Nikon D850 DSLR.
  • Exposure: 1/60 seconds at f/5.6.
  • Special Technique: Long exposure to capture the ethereal water reflection.
